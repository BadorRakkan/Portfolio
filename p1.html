Pathway Genie

Pathway Genie is a web-based tool that reads gene expression data and biological pathways of these genes in order to generate a comprehensive report analyzing the data and producing precise results in a short amount of time. This tool will be useful for geneticists and all researchers. Our interest in pathway detection anal- ysis was sparked by the notion that some bodies can have mutations or diseases while others are healthy. Additionally, finding the first compromised biological pathway might point us in the direction of other compromised biological path- ways and make it easier for geneticists to retrieve data. With the help of using artificial intelligence, a model was successfully built to predict and distinguish between tumor and non-tumor samples with an accuracy of 96%.
