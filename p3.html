London Bike Rides Dashboard

In order to enhance my skills, I started this project to show off my knowledge of data cleaning, visualization, and storytelling. I started the project by actively participating in the data cleaning process, using Python and the Pandas library. After the dataset was completely cleaned, I used Tableau to show the insights that were ready to be revealed.

Through this project, I gained valuable insights into London's bike ride trends:

Interesting seasonality trends were shown by the Total Bike Rides over time graphic, which showed increased ride counts in particular months. The Temperature vs Wind Speed heatmap demonstrated that weather conditions play a role in shaping ride patterns. For the purpose of allocating resources and making plans, the Hour distribution provided insight into the times of peak bike rides.
